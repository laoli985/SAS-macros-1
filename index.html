Analysis of time-to-event endpoints (survival analysis)

STDLOGHR

This macro computes the standardized log hazard ratio, a measure of strength of association with the risk of an event, from a multivariable Cox proportional hazards regression.
Reference: Crager MR (2012). Generalizing the standardized hazard ratio to multivariate proportional hazards regression, with an application to clinical~genomic studies. Journal of Applied Statistics 39:399–417.

STDLOGHR.sas      User guide

STDLOG_INT

This macro computes the standardized log hazard ratio for interactions of treatment (or other randomized intervention) with a set of variables from a multivariable Cox proportional hazards regression.

Discovery analysis

TDRDAS

Starting with a vertical input file containing a list of predictors, their standardized estimated degrees of association with some outcome or state and its estimated standard error, this macro identifies sets of the predictors with strong association with the outcome while controlling the false discovery rate (FDR) and produces estimates of the degree of association corrected for regression to the mean (RM).
Reference: Crager MR (2010). Gene identification using true discovery rate degree of association sets and estimates corrected for regression to the mean. Statistics in Medicine 29:33–45.

TDRDAS.sas      User guide

TDRDAS_SEPCLASS

Starting with a vertical input file containing a list of predictors, standardized estimated degrees of association with some outcome or state, its estimated standard error, and a class identifier dividing the predictors into exhaustive, mutually exclusive classes, this macro identifies sets of the predictors with strong association with the outcome while controlling the false discovery rate (FDR) using Efron's separate class method applied to TDRDA analysis and produces estimates of the degree of association corrected for regression to the mean (RM) within class.
Reference: Crager MR, Ahmed M (2014). Separate Class True Discovery Rate Degree of Association Sets for Biomarker Identification. Journal of Biopharmaceutical Statistics 24:1022-1034.
