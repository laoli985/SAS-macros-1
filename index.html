STDLOGHR

This macro computes the standardized log hazard ratio, a measure of strength of association with the risk of an event, from a multivariable Cox proportional hazards regression.
Reference: Crager MR (2012). Generalizing the standardized hazard ratio to multivariate proportional hazards regression, with an application to clinical~genomic studies. Journal of Applied Statistics 39:399â€“417.

STDLOG_INT

This macro computes the standardized log hazard ratio for interactions of treatment (or other randomized intervention) with a set of variables from a multivariable Cox proportional hazards regression.
